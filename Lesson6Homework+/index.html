Mobile Legends: Bang Bang Overview Mobile Legends: Bang Bang is a popular multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game developed and published by Moonton. Released in 2016, the game is designed primarily for mobile devices and has gained massive popularity worldwide, especially in Southeast Asia. In the game, players are divided into two teams of five and compete to destroy the enemy's base while defending their own. Each player controls a unique hero with special abilities and can choose different roles such as tank, marksman, assassin, mage, support, or fighter. Strategic teamwork, map awareness, and quick reflexes are essential for victory. The game features ranked matches, events, hero skins, and regular updates that introduce new heroes and gameplay changes.
